I'm a Ph.D. student at the Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT), working with Prof. Jingyu Wang and Prof. Jianxin Liao. Before that, I received my B.Eng and M.Sc from BUPT.

My current focus is on 3D Hand Reconstruction, Gesture Recognition, and Video Grounding.
